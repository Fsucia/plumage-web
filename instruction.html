How to Play the Spell Word Game

  • You will see a random image and a hint with a combination of letters.
  • Form and enter as many valid English words as possible using the provided letters.
  • Each valid word increases your score by 1 point.
  • You have 120 seconds to guess as many words as you can.
  • Repeated words won't count again.
  • Once time runs out, no more words can be entered.
